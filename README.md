## 🙋 A little about me

<img align="right" alt="Coding" width="300" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/229223263-cf2e4b07-2615-4f87-9c38-e37600f8381a.gif"/>

You know that feeling when an idea just *clicks* — and you can't stop until it's live?

That's me, every time.

I'm **Kier**, a Full Stack Developer based in the Philippines 🇵🇭. I love the whole journey — from sketching out a UI in Figma, writing clean TypeScript on the frontend, wiring up APIs in the backend, and making sure the whole thing actually *works* before it ships.

- 🎨 I care a lot about how things **look and feel** — good UI/UX isn't a luxury, it's respect for the user
- 🧪 I break my own code before users do (QA mindset 😅)
- ☁️ Currently leveling up in **cloud architecture** — AWS & GCP are my playgrounds
- 🏆 Hackathons? **Yes please.** Nothing like building something real under pressure
- 🤝 Always open to collabs, side projects, and good conversations

> *"The best way to learn is to ship something you're not 100% ready for."*
